Rules

Click "Get Show" to get started and the application will load a random TV show title from the TVMaze API.

From there you will have the option to guess how many seasons and episode the show contains. There are a total of 5 guesses and 3 rounds. After the first guess the hint section will update and tell you if you need to guess higher (+) or lower (-). The border on the input sections will also be changed based on if the guess is correct (green), wrong (red), or within 1 (yellow).

The reset button can be used at any time to bring the game state back to the beginning.

Help

As it is quite hard to guess how many episodes a TV show has you can click the boxes, after one guess to have the answers shown.

WARNING: on subsequent rounds this will only update after the first guess has been made.
